About Me

Collected with intention — rocks, vintage finds, cozy life details, and the Niagara region.

Hi, I’m Rebecca.

I’m a rockhounder with a soft spot for beautiful details — polished edges, old wood, warm light, and the kind of finds that make you stop and smile. I recently moved to St. Catharines this fall and I’m genuinely excited to be in the Niagara region — beaches, trails, and all.

This site is my curated corner: stones I’ve collected, small drops for sale, and a place to keep my favorite moments (and videos) all in one home.

Also: I’m a proud cat mom. My crew includes Olive, Puzzle, Finn, Nala, and Smokey. I also had Caesar, who passed away — and is still part of my story.

Follow along on Instagram: rock.girl.cat.mom

The vibe

Forest green, gold, and a little blush-pink — simple, modern, and quietly expensive. Clean lines. Soft borders. No clutter.
